WOTAN

A social tournaments platform — structured competition, permanent public standings, no gambling vocabulary.

What this is

Tournaments, not lobbies: recurring series, named events, and season archives, built on identity you actually own and a record that's public, permanent, and cite-able. Every entrant starts with an equal stack — no persistent bankroll, no farming, no pay-to-win. This is a real product under active construction, not a mockup: the design doc is public.

How it's built

  • Closed, non-redeemable economy. No deposits, no withdrawals, no secondary markets. Chips and stacks exist only inside a tournament.
  • One identity, one seat. Real accounts, not anonymous tables — your record follows you.
  • Server authority. Rules, RNG, and outcomes are decided server-side and are auditable; clients render and submit input, nothing else.
  • Standings as a shared object. Results are public and permanent, not deleted or reset.
